Wireless debugging android app in your physical android device

Anindra November 25, 2021 Android

After android 11 it is become easy to use wireless debugging from a physical device. But if your device is rooted then can use wireless debugging in a very simple way and it works even your android version of android 11 or more than 11 or less than 11.

Android 11 devices

If your android version is android 11 or above then try this. First, need to enable Developer Options. For this go to Settings then About Phone. After this scroll down android, you will see an option called Build Number tap on it more than 7 times and enter your password, this you will see a toast message 'You are now a developer!', It's mean you are successfully enabled developer option.

Now enable wireless debugging from the developer option, to do this go to device System and scroll down you will see Developer Options click on it and again scroll down slowly and enable USB debugging and enable Wireless debugging and click on it, then one popup option will be called ad 'Allow wireless debugging on this network?' click on allow (remember that your android device and your pc/laptop is connected to the same wifi network). Now on the next screen select Pair device with pairing code, this will show something like this in the screenshot.

There are two options to pair it with your computer, using a QR code or pairing a device using a six-digit code.

Now come to your PC or laptop, for windows devices double click on adb.exe and type this

$ adb pair 192.168.0.267:34568 #this will be defferent in your case
#and after this enter your Wi-Fi pairing code
Enter pairing code: 765467
Successfully paired to 192.168.0.267:34568

For macOS or Linux open the terminal and type this

$ adb connect 192.168.0.267:34568 #this will be defferent in your case
#and after this enter your Wi-Fi pairing code
Enter pairing code: 765467
Successfully paired to 192.168.0.267:34568

Rooted devices

For rooted devices simply download wireless debugging app example Wireless ADB: ADB over TCP/IP, WADB - Simple wireless ADB enabler, ADB Wifi, etc.

After downloading complete open app and enabling wireless, and app will show IP(example 192.168.0.267) this will help to connect via and.

Now open adb.exe in windows device and type this command

adb connect 192.168.0.267

For macOS and Linux

adb connect 192.168.0.267
